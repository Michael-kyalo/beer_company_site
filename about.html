About Us

Brewing Beginnings: The Little Johnny's Genesis

Little Johnny’s origin story is as rich and flavorful as the brews we create. It all began with a chance encounter between a group of friends who shared a love for craft beer and a dream of bringing something unique to their community. Johnny, the spirited visionary behind the venture, had a knack for concocting homebrew recipes that delighted the taste buds of everyone lucky enough to try them. Inspired by Johnny’s passion and the joy it brought to those around him, the friends decided to turn their hobby into something more.

With boundless enthusiasm and a dash of entrepreneurial spirit, they set out to create Little Johnny’s—a place where creativity, camaraderie, and quality craftsmanship would flow as freely as the beer. From humble beginnings in a small garage, they honed their brewing skills, experimenting with different ingredients and techniques to craft the perfect pint. As word of their exceptional brews spread, so too did their ambitions, and before long, Little Johnny’s had become a beloved fixture in the local brewing scene.

But it wasn’t just about the beer; it was about the people who shared in the journey. Each batch brewed was a testament to the friendships forged and the community that rallied behind them. Today, as Little Johnny’s continues to grow and evolve, we remain true to our roots, guided by the same principles that inspired our founders all those years ago. So raise a glass to friendship, to passion, and to the spirit of adventure that fuels every pour at Little Johnny’s. Cheers!

Sip, Share, Celebrate: Discover Little Johnny's

At Little Johnny’s, we believe that great beer is more than just

a beverage; it’s an experience. That’s why each batch we brew is crafted with care, using only the

finest ingredients sourced from local farmers and suppliers whenever possible. From classic ales to

daring experimental brews, our lineup is as diverse as the individuals who enjoy it. Whether you’re

a seasoned beer aficionado or just starting your journey into the world of craft brewing, there’s

something for everyone at Little Johnny’s.
